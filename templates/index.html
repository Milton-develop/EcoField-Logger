EcoField Logger

Designed for fieldwork in the Department of Animal Biology
and Conservation Science

+ Create New Entry View My Data

EcoField Logger is a field data collection platform designed for students working in groups, enabling reliable data capture in low-connectivity environments while ensuring seamless synchronization and organization of collected data. Field data collection is often disrupted by poor internet connectivity and unstructured data entry. EcoField Logger solves this by allowing students to collect and store data offline, while automatically syncing and organizing it once a connection is available. Students simply log in, select their group or project, and start entering field observations using a simple, offline-friendly interface. Once back online, all data syncs automatically to the central database. The collected data can then be analyzed for research projects, lab reports, or group presentations, making EcoField Logger a reliable companion from fieldwork to final reporting.
